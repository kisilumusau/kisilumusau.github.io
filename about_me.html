My Journey into Computer Science

Honestly, this is the first project I have done and the first long code I have written. I am a beginner coder with a strong passion for learning and problem-solving. My journey into coding began with the JKUAT Youth in STEM program I did before I left Kenya, where I am currently learning the fundamentals of programming. With a background as a mathlete in high school, I have always loved numbers and logical reasoning, which naturally led me to explore Computer Science.

My interest in Computer Science stems from my love for challenges and my ability to approach problems analytically. As a former music student, I understand the power of structure and creativity‚Äîtwo essential skills in both music and coding. Whether it's solving a tough algorithm or debugging a stubborn piece of code, I thrive on figuring things out independently before seeking help.

Aspiring to become a Computer Scientist, I am committed to continuously expanding my knowledge and honing my skills. I believe that data holds the key to making informed decisions, and I am excited to be on this journey of learning how to harness its power effectively.

My goals:

  1. To become a proficient Software Engineer and Computer Scientist.
  2. To contribute to projects that make a positive impact on society.
  3. To continuously learn and grow as a developer.

My Personal Story:

I did my highschool at Moi High School-Kabarak where I learnt various subjects including the three sciences and Music. In school I was the Band Master and also Choir Leader which molded me into a leader and a team player. I also participated in the Kenya National Music Festival where I won various awards. After highschool, I joined the JKUAT Youth in STEM program where I was introduced to coding and Computer Science. This program ignited my passion for technology and led me to pursue a degree in Computer Science at Gisma University of Applied Sciences.

My journey has been filled with challenges, but I have always believed in the power of perseverance and hard work. I am excited to continue my studies and explore the endless possibilities that Computer Science has to offer.

In my free time, I enjoy playing the saxophone, reading books on philosophy and going to the gym. I believe that a well-rounded life is essential for personal growth and creativity, and I strive to maintain a balance between my academic pursuits and personal interests.
